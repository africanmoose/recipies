Classic Gin and Tonic

Description

This classic gin and tonic recipie will go down smooth on a hot day, or really any day.

Ingredients

  • 1 tot Gin
  • 200ml tonic
  • ice
  • a sprig of mint

Steps

  1. Add ice to a glass
  2. Bruise the mint lightly and add to the glass
  3. Add the Gin
  4. Top up with tonic
