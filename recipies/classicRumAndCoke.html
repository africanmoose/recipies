Classic Rum and Coke

Description

This classic Rum and Coke recipie will go down smooth on a hot day, or really any day.

Ingredients

  • 1 tot Rum
  • 200ml Coke
  • ice
  • a slice of lemon/lime or Orange for garnish

Steps

  1. Add ice to a glass
  2. Add the slice of lemon to the glass
  3. Add the Rum
  4. Top up with Coke
